Who is the Coderturtle?

The Coderturtle is a creature of curious contradictions: part architect, part chaos monkey. Equal parts caffeine and config files.

This site documents the journey of an AI Architect in the making — fumbling through Terraform states, dodging YAML explosions, and experimenting with platform blueprints that may or may not involve cartoon turtles.

Inspired by the likes of Coding Horror, but with a softer shell and stranger metaphors. If you're here for practical wisdom, war stories, and the occasional ASCII turtle, you're in the right place.
